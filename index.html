About Us

Africa experiences 342 maternal deaths per 100,000 live births. This is 18x the world's average mortality rate.

GluCorp aims to reduce this rate through technology. By providing pregnant women with the tools they need to monitor their health and the health of their baby, we can help them have a healthy pregnancy journey.

Our app makes prenatal and postnatal care more accessible for pregnant mothers in Africa, enabling them to monitor their health effectively.

  • AI-powered urinalysis tests
  • Blood glucose and blood pressure monitoring
  • Baby development tracker
  • Interactive educational chatbot
  • Community forum for sharing experiences
  • Medication reminder system
  • In-app doctor consultations
  • In-app purchases of medication and baby products

Our Vision

Our vision is to transform prenatal and postnatal for pregnant mothers in Africa.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower pregnant mothers with the tools they need to have a healthy pregnancy journey. We believe that every mother deserves the best care possible, and we are committed to making that care available to all. We aim to provide affordable and accessible healthcare to pregnant mothers, ensuring that they have the support they need to have a healthy pregnancy and a healthy baby.
