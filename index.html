About Us

GluCorp is a startup with a vision to transform healthcare through technology. Our app aims to make prenatal and postnatal care more accessible for pregnant mothers, enabling them to keep track of their health.

  • The app features an AI model that can perform urinalysis tests.
  • Close monitoring of blood glucose and blood pressure levels, allowing for early diagnosis.
  • Baby development tracker.
  • An interactive chatbot for educating the women on pregnancy and why they need the care.
  • A community forum for sharing experiences.
  • A reminder system for taking medication.
  • In-app doctor consultations are available for those who need it.
  • In-app purchases of medication and baby products are also available.

Our Vision

Our vision is to transform prenatal and postnatal for pregnant mothers in Africa.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower pregnant mothers with the tools they need to have a healthy pregnancy journey. We believe that every mother deserves the best care possible, and we are committed to making that care available to all. We aim to provide affordable and accessible healthcare to pregnant mothers, ensuring that they have the support they need to have a healthy pregnancy and a healthy baby.
