GluCorp Data Deletion Request

At GluCorp Health Inc., we respect your privacy and give you full control over your data.

How to Request Data Deletion

To request deletion of your data from our app, please:

  1. Send an email to privacy@glucorp.org with the subject line: Data Deletion Request.
  2. Include the email address or phone number linked to your account.
  3. Our support team will verify your request and confirm once your data has been deleted.

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • Profile information (name, email, phone)
  • Health tracking logs (blood pressure, glucose, symptoms, etc.)
  • Chat history
  • Community posts and comments

What Data May Be Retained

  • Legally required records (e.g., tax/payment records)
  • Anonymized, aggregated data (used for research and service improvement)

Retention of required data is limited to the minimum time needed by law.

Questions?

For any questions, contact us at:
